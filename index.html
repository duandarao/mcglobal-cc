📋 Certifications We Support

Our Compliance Coverage

📡
FCC
Federal Communications Commission
Required for any product with radio-frequency components (WiFi, Bluetooth, cellular). We design RF architectures that pass FCC Part 15 and coordinate with accredited test labs.
🧒
CPSIA
Consumer Product Safety Improvement Act
Governs lead content limits, phthalates, and children's product testing requirements. We maintain Children's Product Certificate (CPC) documentation for every project.
🔬
ASTM F963
Standard Consumer Safety Specification for Toy Safety
The comprehensive US toy safety standard covering mechanical, electrical, flammability, and chemical hazards. Our prototype QC process includes pre-compliance ASTM F963 checks.
⚗️
CA Prop 65
California Safe Drinking Water and Toxic Enforcement Act
Requires disclosure of chemicals known to cause cancer or reproductive toxicity. We source compliant materials and manage labeling requirements for California-bound products.
🍁
CCPSA
Canada Consumer Product Safety Act
Canada's federal product safety legislation, including SOR/2011-17 Toys Regulations. We support dual-certification paths for brands targeting both US and Canadian markets.
🔐
COPPA
Children's Online Privacy Protection Act
Critical for any AI product that collects voice, behavioral, or location data from children under 13. We advise on data architecture, consent flows, and parental control mechanisms.

❓ FAQ

Questions We Hear
Every Week

Direct answers, no marketing fluff. If your question isn't here, book a free call.

How does the subscription model actually work? ↓
You subscribe to a tier (Explorer, Builder, or Partner), sign our service agreement via DocuSign, and wire transfer the first payment. Within 48 hours, you're matched with a project manager and given access to the platform. Subscription renews quarterly (Explorer) or annually (Builder/Partner). No auto-billing — you receive an invoice before each renewal.
What is the minimum commitment period? ↓
Explorer is quarter-by-quarter (no annual lock-in). Builder and Partner require an annual commitment because prototyping cycles inherently span multiple months. We don't believe in trapping clients — if you're unhappy after 90 days, we'll discuss a resolution.
What does the cancellation policy look like? ↓
Explorer: Cancel before the next quarter renews, no penalty. Builder/Partner: 60-day written notice required before annual renewal date. Projects already in progress continue until the current milestone. All completed deliverables remain yours.
Is there a free trial? ↓
We offer a free 30-minute discovery consultation (book via Calendly on the Contact page). This isn't a sales call — it's a genuine assessment of whether we're a fit. We'll tell you honestly if your project isn't right for our service.
What AI capabilities can you integrate into a physical product? ↓
Voice interaction (ASR/TTS with custom wake words), vision-based recognition (object, face, gesture), LLM-powered conversation engines, emotion detection, adaptive behavior trees, and on-device edge AI inference. We work with standard platforms (ESP32, Qualcomm, MediaTek) as well as custom PCB designs.
What's the realistic minimum order quantity for production? ↓
Prototyping phase: 1–5 units. Small batch trial production: typically 500–1,000 units depending on component complexity. Mass production: 5,000+ units for most AI hardware product categories. We're transparent about MOQ requirements upfront based on your specific BOM.
Can you really deliver a working prototype in 14 days? ↓
For projects in scope: yes. The 14-day target applies to Builder and Partner tier projects where the brief is clear and components are in stock. Complex projects (custom PCB from scratch, novel AI architectures, moving parts) typically take 3–4 weeks. We'll give you a specific timeline after the discovery call, not a marketing promise.
Who owns the intellectual property we develop together? ↓
By default: you own everything we create for your project — designs, firmware, documentation. We retain no license to use your project deliverables for other clients. The exception is generic reusable modules (standard code libraries, generic mechanical components) that we've developed independently — these remain ours but you receive a perpetual license to use them in your products.
Can you work with competitors in the same product category simultaneously? ↓
Partner tier clients can negotiate exclusive category protection in specific geographic markets — this is a defined add-on option. Builder and Explorer tier clients do not receive exclusivity. We maintain strict information separation between client projects regardless of tier.
How do you protect our design files and confidential information? ↓
Multiple layers: mutual NDA with high breach penalties, encrypted document storage, dynamic watermarking on every deliverable (including invisible steganographic marks), read-only document access vault for sensitive files, full access logging, and device-bound login for platform access. Supplier identities are never disclosed — we act as permanent intermediary.
Which certifications do you support? ↓
FCC (radio frequency), CPSIA (children's product safety), ASTM F963 (toy safety standard), California Prop 65, CCPSA (Canada), and COPPA (data privacy for children under 13). We design products with certification requirements as upfront constraints, coordinate with accredited third-party labs, and manage the certification documentation process.
How long does FCC certification take and who pays? ↓
FCC testing typically takes 4–8 weeks through an accredited lab. Test lab fees are a pass-through cost (not included in subscription). We recommend budgeting $3,000–$8,000 depending on the number of radio modules. We coordinate the submission and manage the lab relationship on your behalf.
What's the exact process from first contact to project kickoff? ↓
① Book free 30-min consultation (Calendly) → ② Zoom discovery call → ③ We send a written scope and pricing proposal within 5 business days → ④ You review, negotiate if needed → ⑤ DocuSign the service agreement → ⑥ Wire transfer → ⑦ Platform access granted within 48 hours → ⑧ PM introduces themselves and schedules the project kickoff call.
How do I communicate with my project team during development? ↓
Builder+ tiers: through the project dashboard with threaded discussions, milestone tracking, and file exchange. Partner tier: additionally via a dedicated Slack channel with 7×24 response commitment. All communication is logged and archived for your reference and ours.
